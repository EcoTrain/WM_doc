In order to edit the diary widget settings, **click and hold** the left mouse button on the desired widget on the diary panel.

Available settings:
1. Size:
1.1. Narrow (half line)
1.2. Wide (whole line)
2. Period of aggregation
2.1. Day
2.2. A week
2.3. Month
